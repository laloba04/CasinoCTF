# 🎰 How to Play Slots

**Goal:** Spin the reels and match symbols across paylines to win.

---

## The Flow

1. **Select your bet amount** using the chip buttons.
2. **Click Spin** — the reels spin and stop on random symbols.
3. **Winning lines** are highlighted automatically.
4. **Winnings are added** to your balance instantly.

Slots is a **solo game** — no room needed, play directly from the lobby.

---

## Symbols

| Symbol | Value |
|--------|-------|
| 💎 Diamond | Highest payout |
| 7️⃣ Seven | High payout |
| 🍒 Cherry | Medium payout |
| 🍋 Lemon | Medium payout |
| 🔔 Bell | Medium payout |
| ⭐ Star | Low payout |
| 🃏 Wild | Substitutes any symbol |
| 🌟 Scatter | Triggers bonus regardless of position |

---

## Paylines

The machine pays when matching symbols line up across a payline (left to right). Multiple paylines can win on the same spin.

---

## Special Symbols

- **Wild** — acts as any symbol to complete a winning line.
- **Scatter** — triggers a bonus payout even if not on a payline.
- **Jackpot** — hit all matching top symbols across all reels for the progressive jackpot.

---

## RTP

This machine runs at approximately **95% RTP** (Return to Player) — for every $100 bet, the expected return is ~$95 over time.

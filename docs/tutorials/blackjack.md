# 🃏 How to Play Blackjack

**Goal:** Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

---

## Card Values

| Card | Value |
|------|-------|
| 2–10 | Face value |
| J, Q, K | 10 |
| Ace | 1 or 11 (whichever benefits you) |

---

## The Flow

1. **Place your bet** — choose chips (10 / 25 / 50 / 100 / 250 / 500) and click Bet.
2. **You get 2 cards** face up. Dealer gets 1 face up, 1 face down.
3. **Choose your action:**
   - **Hit** — draw another card
   - **Stand** — keep your hand and end your turn
   - **Double Down** — double your bet and draw exactly one more card
   - **Split** — if your first 2 cards match, split into 2 separate hands (costs another bet)
4. **Dealer plays** — dealer reveals the hidden card and must hit until reaching 17+.
5. **Result** — closest to 21 without busting wins.

---

## Payouts

| Result | Payout |
|--------|--------|
| Win | 1:1 |
| Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first 2 cards) | 3:2 |
| Push (tie) | Bet returned |
| Bust (over 21) | Lose bet |

---

## Tips

- Always split Aces and 8s.
- Never split 10s or 5s.
- Double Down on 11 when dealer shows 2–10.
- Stand on 17+ against a dealer showing a weak card (2–6).

**This table uses 6 decks (312 cards). Dealer hits on soft 17.**

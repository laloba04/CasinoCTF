# ♠️ How to Play Texas Hold'em

**Goal:** Make the best 5-card poker hand using your 2 hole cards and 5 community cards.

---

## The Flow

### 1. Pre-game
- Each player gets **2 private cards** (hole cards).
- **Small Blind** and **Big Blind** rotate each hand.

### 2. Betting Rounds

| Round | Community Cards Revealed |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **Preflop** | None — bet with just your hole cards |
| **Flop** | 3 cards revealed |
| **Turn** | 1 more card (4 total) |
| **River** | 1 more card (5 total) |
| **Showdown** | Best hand wins the pot |

### 3. Actions per round

- **Fold** — give up your hand, lose any bet
- **Check** — pass without betting (only if no bet yet)
- **Call** — match the current bet
- **Raise** — increase the bet
- **All In** — bet all your chips

---

## Hand Rankings (Best → Worst)

| Hand | Example |
|------|---------|
| Royal Flush | A K Q J 10 (same suit) |
| Straight Flush | 7 8 9 10 J (same suit) |
| Four of a Kind | K K K K 3 |
| Full House | Q Q Q 7 7 |
| Flush | Any 5 of the same suit |
| Straight | 4 5 6 7 8 (any suits) |
| Three of a Kind | J J J 4 2 |
| Two Pair | 9 9 4 4 K |
| One Pair | A A 7 3 2 |
| High Card | Highest card in hand |

---

## Tips

- You need at least 2 players to start a hand.
- The button (dealer position) rotates after each hand.
- Your best 5-card hand is auto-selected from your 2 cards + 5 community cards.
- **Bluffing is part of the game!**

# 🂡 How to Play Baccarat

**Goal:** Bet on whether the Player hand or the Banker hand will be closest to 9.

---

## Card Values

| Card | Value |
|------|-------|
| Ace | 1 |
| 2–9 | Face value |
| 10, J, Q, K | 0 |

When the total exceeds 9, only the **last digit** counts (e.g., 7 + 8 = 15 → hand value is 5).

---

## Bet Options

| Bet | Payout | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| **Player** wins | 1:1 | No commission |
| **Banker** wins | 0.95:1 | 5% commission to house |
| **Tie** | 8:1 | Both hands equal |

> Banker is statistically the best bet (lowest house edge ~1.06%).

---

## The Flow

1. **Choose your bet** — Player, Banker, or Tie — and set the amount.
2. **Click Deal** — two cards each are dealt to Player and Banker.
3. **Third card rule** — drawn automatically based on fixed casino rules:
   - Player draws if total is 0–5.
   - Banker draws based on their total and whether Player drew a third card.
4. **Result** — hand closest to 9 wins.

---

## Tips

- Tie bets have a high payout (8:1) but a very high house edge (~14%). Avoid them.
- Banker wins slightly more than Player over time.
- There's no skill involved — just choose your bet and enjoy the result.

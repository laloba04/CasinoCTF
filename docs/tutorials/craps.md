# 🎲 How to Play Craps

**Goal:** Bet on the outcome of dice rolls. One player rolls (the "shooter"), others bet.

---

## The Flow

### Phase 1: Come Out Roll
The shooter rolls both dice for the first time.

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 7 or 11 | **Win** — Pass Line bets win |
| 2, 3, or 12 (Craps) | **Lose** — Pass Line bets lose |
| Any other number | That number becomes the **Point** |

### Phase 2: Point Phase
If a Point was established, the shooter keeps rolling until:
- **Hits the Point again** → Pass Line wins
- **Rolls a 7** → Pass Line loses (seven out)

---

## Bet Types

| Bet | When it wins | Payout |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| **Pass Line** | Come Out 7/11, or Point before 7 | 1:1 |
| **Don't Pass** | Come Out 2/3, or 7 before Point | 1:1 |
| **Come** | Like Pass Line, after Point is set | 1:1 |
| **Don't Come** | Opposite of Come | 1:1 |
| **Field** | Next roll is 2, 3, 4, 9, 10, 11, 12 | 1:1 (2:1 on 2 or 12) |
| **Any Seven** | Next roll is exactly 7 | 4:1 |
| **Any Craps** | Next roll is 2, 3, or 12 | 7:1 |

---

## The Flow in this App

1. **Place bets** — choose a bet type and amount, click the bet button.
2. **Click Roll Dice** — results are shown immediately.
3. **Collect winnings** and bet again for the next roll.
4. Multiple players can bet on the same roll simultaneously.

---

## Tips

- Pass Line / Don't Pass have the lowest house edge (~1.4%) — best bets for beginners.
- Any Seven has a high house edge (~16%) — avoid it.
- Come and Don't Come behave like Pass/Don't Pass but placed mid-game.

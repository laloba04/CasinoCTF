# 🎡 How to Play Roulette

**Goal:** Predict where the ball will land on the spinning wheel (numbers 0–36).

This table uses **European Roulette** (single zero) — house edge 2.7%.

---

## Bet Types

### Inside Bets (higher risk, higher reward)

| Bet | Description | Payout |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Straight Up | One specific number | 35:1 |
| Split | Two adjacent numbers | 17:1 |
| Street | Three numbers in a row | 11:1 |
| Corner | Four numbers in a square | 8:1 |
| Line | Six numbers (two rows) | 5:1 |

### Outside Bets (lower risk, lower reward)

| Bet | Description | Payout |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Red / Black | Color of the number | 1:1 |
| Even / Odd | Parity of the number | 1:1 |
| Low (1–18) / High (19–36) | Range | 1:1 |
| Dozen | 1–12, 13–24, or 25–36 | 2:1 |
| Column | One of 3 columns | 2:1 |

> **Note:** 0 (zero) is neither red nor black, neither odd nor even. All outside bets lose on 0.

---

## The Flow

1. **Select your bet amount** using the chip buttons.
2. **Click a bet type** on the board to place your bet.
3. **Click Spin** — the wheel spins and the ball lands on a number.
4. **Collect winnings** or place a new bet for the next round.

Multiple players can bet simultaneously. The spin happens once all bets are placed.

---

## Tips

- Outside bets win almost half the time — good for beginners.
- Straight up bets pay the most but win rarely.
- You can place multiple different bets in the same round.
